How are you feeling?

If things feel heavy right now, I want you to know it’s okay to feel whatever is coming up. It takes real strength to acknowledge when things aren’t easy, and you’re showing a lot of courage just by being here and being honest about how you feel. It’s okay if you don’t have all the answers or if you’re struggling to make sense of things. Sometimes, just getting through the day is an accomplishment in itself.

Take this moment to be gentle with yourself. You don’t have to carry everything alone, and you don’t have to have it all figured out. There are people who genuinely care about you, who want to listen, and who can help lighten the load when you’re ready. Even when things seem cloudy, know that brighter moments are possible, and you’re deserving of peace and support every step of the way.

For now, it’s enough to just be here, one small step at a time.
